About Me


Hello my name is Gio Ospina and thank you for visiting my portfolio!


I am a team-orientated game developer, level designer, programmer, and a UI/UX designer. After graduating from the Raritan Valley Community College (RVCC) Game Development program, I knew working within tech or the video game industry was something I strive to achieve. Since then, I graduated from The College of New Jersey (TCNJ) where I not only continued my specialization in game design, but also began exploring creativity through different forms of media, graduating summa cum laude with my Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in Interactive Multimedia.
Through my proficiency and understanding of numerous coding languages (C++ / C# / Java / HTML), along with multiple computer programs and software (Adobe Creative Suite / Unity 3D / Unreal Engine / Autodesk Maya), I have been able to develop both 2D and 3D games, 3D models, animations, responsive websites, edited gameplay videos, and user interfaces. While my passion lives with video games, I am a huge Funko Pop collector, a master LEGO Helmet builder, a Disney fanatic, and an avid sports fan supporting my beloved Knicks, West Ham, and Real Madrid!
