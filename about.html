About Me


Hello my name is Giovanny Ospina and welcome to my portfolio website.

I am a TCNJ transfer student who commutes almost an hour away and major in Interactive Multimedia. I am extremely passionate about video games, sports, Disney and animated cartoon shows. I am saddened to say that I am a New York Knicks and Giants fan but it all levels out by being a longtime Real Madrid fan.

My goal with this website is to use it as a motivator as well as a building block towards my hopeful career in either game design or video editing. I intend to publish most if not all of my work that I have done in the past to show my development and growth within my projects as I am just starting out. I used to have all my projects laying around in my dropbox or even several usb sticks, so I thought to myself, why not create a nice simplistic website to showcase some of my works! I feel like I can really progress into the next step in my journey as an IMM student and put my coding, editing, and somehwat artistic skills into play to bring creative ideas into life.
